=== SEGMENT 8 ===
[Summary: The European Union operates through three main bodies: the European Commission, the European Parliament, and the Council of the European Union. The European Commission is responsible for suggesting new laws, which are then discussed and decided upon jointly by the European Parliament (elected by European citizens) and the Council of the European Union (comprising national decision-makers). If a law is approved by both the Parliament and the Council, all EU countries must work to implement it.]
